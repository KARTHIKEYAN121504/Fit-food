About us

Fit and Food Tamil is a homegrown sustainable herbal products company, founded by Abdul Karim Razaak in 2021, that provides high-quality food products, skin and hair care products that enriches our skin, hair, mind and body.

We’re dedicated to bringing joy and hope to continue doing so for years to come. However, we offer more than just high-quality, delicious herbal products.

We are a leading herbal store in Madurai, working with a dedicated team to provide the finest food products to our growing community.

OUR VISION

To be a vehicle for global awareness by creating a holistic, sustainable business model that inspires, promotes, and supports true wellness, respect for all beings, and Mother Nature.

OUR MISSION

To be a truthful and innovative global leader in providing genuine products and solutions for conscious, healthy living.

OUR VALUES

Service to all, total integrity, absolute commitment to quality, respect and devotion to Mother Nature, and no compromise on being who we are.

OUR COMMITMENT

To deliver genuine products at affordable prices to our consumers. To introduce a unique and successful business model that is committed to service and integrity, and benefits all.
